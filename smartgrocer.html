Case Study

SmartGrocer

Overview

A grocery-planning case about making repeated household decisions feel lighter and easier to manage.

My role

Product design, UI, UX, prototyping.

Timeline

2025

Project Takeaways

What I explored

How grocery planning could feel less like admin by supporting repeated habits, saved patterns, and faster edits.

What I learned

Planning tools need to reduce work over time. If every week starts from zero, the product becomes another chore.

What I'd do differently now

I would test the repeat-use loop earlier and design more intentionally around shared household decisions.
