Case Study

FilterNews

Overview

A news-consumption case about reducing overload and helping people find a calmer information rhythm.

5 min read

My role

Product design, UI, UX, prototyping.

Timeline

05/2026

Project Takeaways

What I explored

How news consumption could feel less overwhelming by giving people stronger filters, pacing, and hierarchy.

What I learned

Reducing cognitive load is not only about removing content. It is also about better defaults and clearer decision points.

What I'd do differently now

I would prototype the filtering model earlier and test whether people understand what gets hidden, prioritised, or delayed.
